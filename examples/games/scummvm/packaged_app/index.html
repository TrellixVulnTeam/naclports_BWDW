ScummVM Native Client Demo

This is a Native Client port of ScummVM.

The following games are available in the port (all freeware):

  • Beneath a Steel Sky
  • Lure of the Temptress

Known issues:

  • BASS and Lure crash on saved games for 64-bit machines

Notes:

  • F5 to save/restore games in BASS
  • F5 to save games in Lure, F7 to restore games in Lure


Special thanks to Revolution Games for providing both Beneath a Steel Sky and Lure of the Temptress as freeware!

To select a game, click on Add game.... From the directory, go up two directories to /usr/local and then go to /usr/local/share/scummvm-1.2.1. Then go to a game's directory and click Choose followed by OK. To begin the game, select the directory from the main menu and click Start.

ScummVM may take a few moments to load. Please be patient.
